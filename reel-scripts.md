## General Reel Tips

1. **First 1 second decides everything** — hook hard or they scroll
2. **Add captions** — 85% of IG users watch without sound
3. **Vertical only** — 9:16 aspect ratio
4. **15-30 seconds** — shorter = higher completion rate = more reach
5. **Post at 8-9 PM Sun-Thu or 10-11 AM Fri-Sat** (Qatar peak)
6. **Use trending audio** — check IG Reels tab for what's trending this week
7. **End with a CTA** — "Link in bio" or "DM us PARTY"
