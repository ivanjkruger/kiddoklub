## Daily Story Framework

**Sun–Wed (Weekdays):** 3 stories/day
1. Morning: Behind-the-scenes or teaser for today's post
2. Afternoon: Repost today's feed post with "New post!" sticker
3. Evening: Poll, question box, or countdown to next event
