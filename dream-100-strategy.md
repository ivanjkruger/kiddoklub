# KiddoKlub — Dream 100 Partnership Strategy
> Find the 100 people in Doha who already have your customers, and partner with them.

---

## What is Dream 100?

The Dream 100 concept (from Chet Holmes, popularized by Alex Hormozi) is simple: **stop chasing individual customers and instead partner with the people who already have rooms full of them.**

For KiddoKlub, that means finding the event planners, nurseries, cake makers, photographers, and influencers in Doha who already serve parents of young kids — and making it easy and profitable for them to recommend you.

**One partnership with a busy event planner = 5-10 bookings/month.**
That's more valuable than 1,000 Instagram followers.

## The 10 Target Categories

### 1. Party & Event Planners

**Why they matter:** They're already being hired to plan kids' birthdays. If they recommend you, it's a done deal — the parent trusts their planner.

**What to offer:**
- QAR 150 referral fee per booking they send
- Or 10% commission on the package price
- Bundle deal: their decor + your play zone = one-stop party package
- Free demo at their next event

**How to find them in Doha:**
- Instagram: search #dohapartyplanner #qatarevents #dohaevents #kidspartyplanner
- Google: "party planner Doha" "event planner Qatar kids"
- Ask in mom groups: "Who did your party?"

### 2. Kids' Cake Makers & Bakeries

**Why they matter:** Every birthday has a cake. The cake maker often has a relationship with the parent and gets asked "do you know anyone who does X?"

**What to offer:**

- QAR 100 referral fee per booking
- Joint "party bundle" social media posts

**How to find them in Doha:**
- Instagram: #dohacakes #qatarcakes #kidscakedoha #birthdaycakedoha #customcakesqatar
- Check who mom groups recommend for cakes

**Sample pitch:**

I was thinking our services would complement each other perfectly — your beautiful cake + our play zone = the ultimate birthday. Would you be open to cross-promoting? I'd love to feature your cakes in our setup photos, and I can refer our clients to you too. Let me know if you'd be interested in chatting!

- Flyer for their community board
- They get credit for organizing a fun event — you get 50+ parent leads

**How to find them in Doha:**
- Google: "villa compounds Doha families"
- Known compounds: The Pearl, West Bay, Al Waab, Ain Khaled area compounds
- Facebook: Doha compound community groups

**Sample pitch:**

We'll deliver, set up a full play zone, and let the kids enjoy it for 2 hours, completely free. It's a great activity for the compound and we'll leave our info for any parents who want to book for their own events. Would you be interested?

## Partnership Models (Pick One Per Partner)

| Model | How It Works | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Referral Fee** | QAR 100-200 per booking they send you | Event planners, decorators, cake makers |
| **% Commission** | 10% of booking value | Planners with frequent referrals |
| **Cross-Promotion** | Feature each other on social media | Photographers, influencers, stores |
| **Bundle Deal** | Sell a combined package (their service + yours) | Event planners, balloon decorators |
| **Free Demo** | Set up free at their venue/event | Nurseries, compounds, hotels |
| **Affiliate Code** | They share a unique code, get QAR 100 per use | Influencers, bloggers |

## Weekly Action Plan

### Week 1: Foundation
- [ ] Create tracking spreadsheet
- [ ] Research and list 20 targets across categories (2 per category)
- [ ] Send 10 outreach messages (highest-value categories first: event planners, nurseries, influencers)
- [ ] Follow all 20 targets on Instagram and engage with their content (like, comment, share)

### Week 2: Momentum
- [ ] Follow up on Week 1 outreach (anyone who didn't reply)
- [ ] Send 10 more outreach messages (next batch)
- [ ] Research and add 20 more targets to the list
- [ ] Engage daily with all targets' content

### Week 3: Deepen
- [ ] Follow up on all pending outreach
- [ ] Schedule first free demo (nursery or compound)
- [ ] Book first influencer collaboration
- [ ] Send 10 more outreach messages

### Week 4: Systematize
- [ ] Review what's working — which categories are responding best?
- [ ] Double down on top 2-3 categories
- [ ] Send 10 more outreach messages
- [ ] Goal: have at least 3 active partnerships generating referrals

### Ongoing (Every Week):
- [ ] Send 10 new outreach messages
- [ ] Follow up on all pending conversations
- [ ] Engage with all partners' and targets' content daily
- [ ] Track referral bookings from each partner

## The Goal

**10 active partners sending you 1-2 referrals each per month = 10-20 bookings/month.**

That's going from 1 rental/month to 10-20x — without running a single ad. Just relationships, value exchange, and hustle.
