Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know before booking

How long is the rental?
All packages include 4 hours of play time. Need more? Just let us know and we can arrange extended time.
Do you deliver everywhere in Doha?
Yes! We deliver to all areas of Doha and surrounding areas. Delivery, setup (90 min before your event), and pickup are all included in the price.
What about the QAR 500 deposit?
The deposit is fully refundable. We collect it at delivery and return it at pickup, as long as all equipment is in good condition. Normal wear and tear is expected!
Is it safe for toddlers?
Absolutely! All our soft play equipment is designed for children ages 1-6. Everything is foam-padded, and we include full floor matting and safety fencing. We sanitize all equipment before every rental.
Can I add the Jumping Castle to any package?
Right now it's FREE with every booking! Just mention it when you message us on WhatsApp. The jumping castle is 2.5m x 2.5m and fits perfectly alongside the soft play set.
How much space do I need?
Our Mini package needs just 2m x 2m. Our Large package needs 5m x 4m. Most living rooms and gardens in Doha work perfectly. Send us your space dimensions on WhatsApp and we'll recommend the best fit.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 3-5 days in advance, especially for weekends. Last-minute bookings may be possible depending on availability.
