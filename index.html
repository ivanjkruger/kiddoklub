Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know before booking

Where do you deliver?
We deliver and set up anywhere in Doha, Qatar. Delivery fees may apply depending on location.
How long does setup take?
Our team has everything set up in about 90 minutes. Pack up takes about 1 hour. We handle it all — you don't need to do a thing.
What ages are the soft play sets suitable for?
Our sets are designed for children ages 1–6, though older kids enjoy them too! The soft foam pieces are safe and age-appropriate.
Is there a deposit?
Yes, a QAR 500 refundable deposit in case of damages. Returned in full after the rental if everything is in good condition.
Can I customize my setup?
Absolutely! You can mix and match pieces, add extras, or request a custom arrangement. Just let us know your vision.
What's included with every package?
Delivery, full setup, foam play mats, all listed equipment, and optional white fencing to enclose the play area. We also handle teardown and pickup.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend 3–5 days in advance. Weekend slots fill up fast! But we can sometimes accommodate last-minute requests.
Can I set this up indoors?
Yes! Our sets work great indoors or outdoors. Many families set up in their living room, garage, or villa garden.
