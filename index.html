FAQ

Common Questions

How long is the rental?
All packages include 4 hours of play time. Need more? Let us know and we can arrange extended time.
Do you deliver everywhere in Doha?
Yes! We deliver to all areas of Doha. Delivery, setup (90 min before), and pickup are all included in the price.
What about the QAR 500 deposit?
Fully refundable. We collect it at delivery and return it at pickup, as long as equipment is in good condition.
Is it safe for toddlers?
Absolutely. All equipment is designed for ages 1–6, foam-padded, with full floor matting and safety fencing. Everything is sanitized before each rental.
How much space do I need?
Mini needs 2m × 2m, Large needs 5m × 4m. Most Doha living rooms and gardens work perfectly. Send us your dimensions on WhatsApp.
How far in advance should I book?
3–5 days recommended, especially for weekends. Last-minute bookings possible depending on availability.
